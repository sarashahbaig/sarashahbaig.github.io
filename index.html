The balochi dress is appealing, in form, great and extremely rich that a lady from any piece of world can’t deny wearing it. Balochi dress is called “phashik” in balochi dialect. The attire of the general population of Baluchistan, Pakistan comprises of different styles of kameez and shalwar, turban, shoes and head scarfs.
